3 of my top favorite and most skilled players to play for the Chicago Bears

  1. Brian Urlacher
      Brian Urlacher was one of the best middle linebackers in the NFL for a very long time.
      His bone-crushing tackles and tight coverage ability dominated the league for many years.
      He was a tremendous leader and a great teammate and was beloved by everyone he played with.
    • Hall of Fame (2018)
    • Defensive Player of the Year (2005)
    • 8x Pro Bowler
  2. Charles Tillman
      Charles Tillman was the epitome of the old fashioned iron man. He rarely missed a game and was always willing to suit up with bumps and bruiuses for his team.
      His signature "peanut punch" was recognized around the league for his ability to force fumbles.
      Along with his turnover forcing hand, he was great in coverage and always a willing tackler.
    • 2x Pro Bowler
    • Walter Payton Man of the Year (2013)
    • Has the "Peanut Punch" named after him
  3. Brandon Marshall
      Brandon Marshall standing at 6'4 and running a 4.6 40 was always a freak of nature. He was an amazing jump ball player and had a great run after catch ability.
      In one of his best seasons he lead the league in receptions and touchdowns and had to regularly be covered by multiple people to limit his abilities.
      His production, even while playing with some not so great QB's, was always an admirable stat for him.
    • 6x Pro Bowler
    • Top WR in the league for many years
    • Lead the league in receiving touchdowns 1x
